What it does

Every way to make
your phone suffer.

👊
Slap Detection

Hit the back of your phone and it instantly plays a pain sound. Adjustable sensitivity — from a light tap to a full slap.

👻
Background Mode

Runs completely in the background. Hand the phone to someone — they'll have no idea until they slap it.

🔌
Charger Prank

Plug in the charger and the phone moans. Leave it on someone's desk and watch the chaos unfold.

⏱️
Moan Timer

Set a timer and the phone screams on its own every X minutes. Perfect for haunting a room without being there.

🎙️
Multiple Voices

Choose from different voice packs. Each one has a unique reaction — pick the one that fits the prank.

📳
Haptic Feedback

The phone vibrates in proportion to the force of the slap. The harder you hit, the more it reacts.

Inspired by Slap Your Mac — the viral app that made MacBooks scream when slapped. We brought the same chaotic energy to Android. 📱💥
