PRIVACY
POLICY

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Slap My Phone ("the app") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

1. Information We Collect

Slap My Phone does not directly collect any personal information. However, the app uses third-party services that may collect certain data:

  • Accelerometer data — used locally on your device to detect slaps. This data is never transmitted or stored.
  • Advertising data — collected by Google AdMob to serve ads. This may include your device's Advertising ID.

2. How We Use Information

The app uses your device's accelerometer solely to detect physical interactions (slaps) and trigger sounds and vibration. No data is sent to our servers.

Advertising data is used by Google AdMob to display relevant ads within the app.

3. Third-Party Services

The app integrates the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob — used to display banner advertisements. AdMob may collect and process data according to Google's privacy policy. You can review it at: policies.google.com/privacy

4. Advertising ID

The app uses Google's Advertising ID (GAID) through the AdMob SDK to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. Users in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, and Switzerland will be asked for consent before personalized ads are shown, in accordance with GDPR requirements.

You can reset or opt out of interest-based advertising at any time through your device settings under Google → Ads.

5. Permissions

The app requests the following device permissions:

  • Accelerometer — to detect slaps and physical interactions.
  • Vibration — to provide haptic feedback when a slap is detected.
  • Notifications — to show a persistent notification when the background service is active.
  • Foreground Service — to keep slap detection running while the app is in the background.
  • Battery Optimization Exemption — optional, to ensure reliable background operation.

6. Data Retention

We do not store any personal data on our servers. App preferences (selected voice, sensitivity settings) are stored locally on your device and can be cleared by uninstalling the app.

7. Children's Privacy

Slap My Phone is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

8. Your Rights (EEA/UK Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, you have the following rights under GDPR:

  • The right to access your personal data
  • The right to request deletion of your personal data
  • The right to object to processing of your personal data
  • The right to withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights or for any privacy-related questions, please contact us at the email below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
